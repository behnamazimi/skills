---
name: grill-everything
description: Grill the user relentlessly about a plan, opinion, or claim on any topic. Use when the user wants their thinking stress-tested, before acting on it or just to sharpen it.
disable-model-invocation: true
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the reasoning, resolving dependencies between points one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time, waiting for feedback on each question before continuing. Asking multiple questions at once is bewildering.

If a *fact* can be found by looking it up (codebase, docs, web), do that rather than asking me. The *opinions* and *decisions*, though, are mine — put each one to me and wait for my answer.

Do not concede or move on until I confirm we have reached a shared understanding.
